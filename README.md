WarioWare Game
A microgame collection built with Godot 4, inspired by Nintendo's WarioWare series. Survive a relentless gauntlet of fast-paced mini-games that get harder the longer you play.
How to Play
You have 5 lives (the garlic icons). Each round, a random microgame is thrown at you with a countdown timer. Complete the objective to survive; fail and you lose a life. Lose all 5 and it's game over.
Every round gets faster so make sure to focus.
Microgames
Click the Garlics	Click all 5 garlic cloves scattered on screen
Flappy Bird	Survive by flapping through pipes
Find Luigi	Click Luigi hiding among 50 bouncing Yoshis
Pong	Keep the ball alive by blocking with your paddle
Space Invaders	Shoot all 15 aliens before time runs out
Platformer Climber	Climb endlessly on platforms while avoiding rising lava
Controls
- Mouse - primary control for all games
- WASD for movement when applicable
- ESC -  quit (in-game)
Features
- 6 unique microgames with WarioWare-style presentation
- Ramping difficulty — speed increases each round
- High score tracking (persisted locally)
- Volume and fullscreen settings
- Game over screen with score, highscore, and retry button
